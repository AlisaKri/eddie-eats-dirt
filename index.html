Stop Eating Dirt, Eddie

In standard tuning, the guitar strings are tuned to the notes E, A, D, G, B, E. The playful phrase "Eddie Ate Dirt, Good Bye Eddie" helps to remember this sequence.

This game will train you to remember the note of each string without reciting this phrase over and over again, so poor Eddie can finally stop eating dirt.
